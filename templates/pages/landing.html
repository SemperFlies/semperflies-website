SemperFlies

We are a Non Profit Orginization, 501(c)(3) pending, in the South Lake Tahoe area of California. We participate in, support, & host events raising awareness for Veterans that struggle with PTSD, TBI, and Depression. 100% of the information we provide “is not” from any clinical background we have but rather from our own life experiences battling these conditions. We are not licensed in mental health and encourage anyone struggling with these conditions to seek professional help. We are committed to doing anything we can to Educate, Advocate, & Support any Veteran that served our country.

"Stay in The Fight!"

-Semper Flies Foundation

Our Mission

  • E.A.S - Educate, Advocate and Support Veterans suffering from PTSD, TBI & Depression
  • Honor Cpl. Jason David Mileo
  • Support Other Veteran 501(c)(3)'s
