SemperFlies

We are a non profit based in the Carson Valley area, for veterans by veterans. We host events, provide mental health services and support veterans in any way we can.

Our Mission

  • E.A.S.E - Educate, Advocate and Support Veterans suffering from PTSD, TBI & Depression
  • Honor Cpl. Jason David Mileo
  • Support Other Veteran 501(c)(3)'s
